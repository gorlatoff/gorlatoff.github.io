    • English version

Teksty s korektorami (Texts that were proofread):

  • "Turistične konversacije" (an Interslavic language phrasebook). Certainly, the phrases from this phrasebook are the most understandable of all texts that exist in Interslavic. All problematic parts were discusssed and corrected by people from various nations.

  • Texts of Melac's songs (a known Interslavic creator).

  • "Čitateljnik" anthology (2018). It contains 36 different texts in one hundred pages.

Reading:

  • Medžuviki (Developing encyclopedia in Interslavic). It has 446 articles.

  • The corpus of Interslavic texts, it contains over a thousand texts from dozens of authors.

  • A translation of the book "The Little Prince".

  • Texts collection by Jan van Steenbergen. It includes 14 sample texts that were added in 2006 and later.

  • A translation of part of the chapters of Jacob Perelman's book "Physics at every Step".

  • Text collection in Medžuviki: Poems • Articles • Books • Jokes • Stories • Songs • Prayers • Quotes and proverbs • News and olds • Learning Interslavic • Other texts

  • Translations of songs.

  • "Dove Book". The Dove Book is a medieval historical document - a religious verse, written somewhere in the 1500's. A Russian legend of the creation of the world.

  • "Diky vepr" comic ("Wild pig").

  • "Rastuči větr" ("The growing wind").

  • Card Game "NOVY-2084" .

  • Religious texts by Miroslav Vančo: Dekalog • Ave Maria • Pater noster • Gloria Patri • Rozvěnec Děvice Marije • Apostolsky symbol věry
